# Artisan Cafe — 2-Minute Demo Script

## Opening (15 seconds)
> "Hey [name], I built something specifically for cafes like yours. Let me show you how it works — it takes 2 minutes."

*Open your phone, navigate to the live Vercel URL.*

---

## The Menu Experience (30 seconds)
> "Here's the full menu — beautifully laid out, fast-loading images, filterable by category."

*Tap Coffee → Food → Seasonal to show filtering.*

> "Your customers can browse everything from their phone while they're walking over. See this 'Seasonal' tag? When lavender season ends, you mark it sold out from your dashboard. One click."

---

## The Order Flow (45 seconds)
> "Now watch this — I'll add a couple items to the cart."

*Add Oat Milk Latte (×2) and Avocado Toast.*

> "Cart updates in real time. Now I tap Checkout."

*Walk through: Review → Pickup Time → Payment.*

> "The customer picks their own pickup time. No phone calls. No misheard orders. They pay right here, and it shows up on your end instantly."

---

## The Owner Dashboard (20 seconds)
> "And this is what you see."

*Navigate to /admin, enter password.*

> "Pending orders, one-tap 'Mark Ready'. Revenue for the day right at the top. No training needed — your barista can run this."

---

## The Close (10 seconds)
> "Right now, DoorDash takes 30% of every delivery order. This platform is a flat $199/month — every dollar goes to you. Want me to set it up for your cafe this week?"

---

## Tips
- Demo on your **phone**, not a laptop. Cafe owners think mobile-first.
- Have a **real credit card** loaded in test mode so the Stripe/Razorpay animation looks real.
- If they ask "Can you add X?" — always say yes. You can.
